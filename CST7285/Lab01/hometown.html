Welcome to Weifang

City Information

  • Weifang is a prefecture-level city in central Shandong province, People's Republic of China.
  • The city borders Dongying to the northwest, Zibo to the west.
  • Its population was 9,086,241 at the 2010 census, of whom 2,659,938 lived in the built-up (or metro) area.

Delicious Food in Weifang

  1. Weifang Baozi - A type of stuffed bun that is a local specialty.
  2. Weifang Shaoji - A traditional chicken dish that is well-known throughout the region.
  3. Weifang Sausage - A unique local sausage that is a must-try for any food lover.
  4. Weifang Noodles - A popular local noodle dish with a rich and flavorful sauce.
